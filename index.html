Who we are

About
Us

Ale Skate Club is a Warsaw-based skateboarding community that has been bringing together skaters from all over the city since 2022.

It doesn't matter what you ride: a classic deck, a surfskate, longboard, dancing, or even that old board you use to grab groceries. If it has wheels, it fits right in.

If you can stand on your board and ride straight, come join us! Whether you're just starting out or already landing tricks, everyone is welcome.

Our main season runs from April to September, whenever the weather lets us turn Warsaw into our playground. You can find us several times a week cruising by the Vistula river, hanging at local squares, or hitting the skateparks.

We also pull off special events like day & night city rides with a police escort, or crew trips to Skarland — the biggest skatepark in Poland.

Just show up with your board — no ego, no skill-judging — just pure stoke and the joy of riding together!

Our meetups are 100% free and we use English in our official communication because we want everyone to feel welcome — including the international community living in Warsaw.

When we skate

Regular
Meetings

Every Tuesday
ALE Tuesday
Polna 30a / Politechnika metro → Letnisko Żoliborz

Our core weekday session and the one that brings together the biggest part of the community. If you want to meet as many people from the ALE Skate crew as possible, this is the one.

We start at Polna 30a, next to Politechnika metro, ride toward the Vistula, cruise the riverbanks, and finish at Letnisko Żoliborz — deckchairs, drinks, and catching up. Everyone rides at their own pace.

→ WhatsApp
Various weekdays
ALE Dance
Plac Piłsudskiego / National Stadium area

Focused on longboard dancing — longer boards, flow, style, and movement. Practice, learn, and skate in a more expressive way.

Sessions fall on different weekdays depending on conditions. You'll find experienced riders and complete beginners. No matter your level, you're welcome. Ask in the WhatsApp group when the next dance session is.

→ WhatsApp
Every Saturday · Morning onward
ALE Saturday
Zielonka Skatepark · Dziennikarska 57

Weekly community meetups at the skatepark in Zielonka, every Saturday from the morning, all season.

Skaters and beginners ride together; sometimes people show up just to hang out. We ride, talk, learn from each other. First time at a skatepark? There's usually someone to show you basics and cheer you on.

→ WhatsApp

Our main season runs April–September, but exact dates always depend on the weather. Session times shift throughout the season. Always check our WhatsApp group to confirm the current spot and exact time before heading out.

Beyond the weekly

Special
Events

Weekend Camp · 3–4× per season
ALE Surfskate Camp in Skarland
Free Skarżysko-Kamienna Biggest park in Poland

Free weekend surfskate camps at the largest skatepark in Poland in Skarżysko-Kamienna (Skarland).

We usually leave Friday after work or early Saturday and spend the whole weekend at the park. Days: riding and skating together. Evenings: campfire, tents on the park grounds. Good weather: swims in a nearby lake.

3–4 trips per season — dates announced before the season starts.

City Event · 2× per season
ALE Skateboarding Street Parade
Free Warsaw streets Up to ~150 riders

Our biggest seasonal event — often the largest turnout of the year.

With the City of Warsaw: we skate as a group through streets that are normally for cars, where skating usually isn't allowed — safely, with a police escort.

Not a race: a relaxed cruise in a big group (dozens, up to ~150 people). Any board welcome — street, surfskate, longboard.

Roughly 2 times per season.
