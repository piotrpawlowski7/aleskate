Who we are

About
Us

Ale Skate Club is a Warsaw-based skateboarding community that has been bringing together skaters from all over the city since 2022.

Ale Skate are skateboarders, longboarders, surfskaters, board-dancers, downhillers, wheelpushers, flow cruisers... and all in-between.

We're doing regular meet-ups, ride-alongs, and skate sessions in Warsaw, as well as skate-trips outside the city. We care about people and building good community as much (if not more!) as skateboarding sports.

It's a part of the lifestyle, of the experience, and a way to spend your time, together. We welcome everyone who rides or skates any boards (with wheels), and would like to share that feeling with us.

If you can stand on your board and ride straight, come join us! Whether you're just starting out or already landing tricks, everyone is welcome.

Our main season runs from April to September. You can find us several times a week cruising by the river, through the city, hanging at local squares, or hitting the skateparks.

We also pull off special events like day & night closed, big rides through the middle of the most iconic Warsaw streets!

Our meetups are 100% free to join and we use English in our official channels, so there's no language barrier between us and skating. Join us!

When we skate

Regular
Meetings

Every Tuesday
ALE.TUESDAYS
Polna 30a / Politechnika metro → Letnisko Żoliborz

Our core weekly ride and the one that brings together the biggest part of the community. If you want to meet as many people from the ALE Skate crew as possible, this is the one.

We start at Polna 30a, next to Politechnika metro, ride toward the Vistula, cruise the riverbanks, and usually finish at Letnisko Żoliborz for afterride drinks and catching up. Everyone rides at their own pace.

→ WhatsApp
Various weekdays
ALE.DANCE
Plac Piłsudskiego / National Stadium area

Focused on longboard dancing: sessions to learn, train and hone some cool dance steps and tricks on longboards.

Meetups fall on different weekdays depending on conditions. You'll find experienced dancers and complete beginners. You don't know how but want to try? Come and someone will show you the basics. Check or ask in the WhatsApp sub-group when the next dance session is.

→ WhatsApp
Every Saturday · Morning onward
ALE.SATURDAYS
Zielonka Skatepark · Dziennikarska 57

Weekly community meetups at the skatepark in Zielonka (north-east of the city), aimed mainly at surfskaters and skateboarders, every Saturday from the morning, all season.

Skaters and beginners ride together; sometimes people show up just to hang out. We ride, talk, learn from each other. First time at a skatepark? There's usually someone to give you tips on how to start and cheer you on.

→ WhatsApp

Our main season runs April–September, but exact dates always depend on the weather. Session times shift throughout the season. Always check our WhatsApp Community to confirm the current spot and exact time before heading out.

Beyond the weekly

Special
Events

Weekend Camp · 3–4× per season
ALE Surfskate Camp in Skarland
Free Skarżysko-Kamienna Biggest park in Poland

Free weekend surfskate camps at the largest skatepark in Poland in Skarżysko-Kamienna (Skarland).

We usually leave Friday after work or early Saturday and spend the whole weekend at the park. Days: riding and skating together. Evenings: campfire, tents on the park grounds. Good weather: swims in a nearby lake.

3–4 trips per season — dates announced before the season starts.

City Event · 1–2× per season
ALE Skateboarding Street Parade
Free Warsaw streets Up to ~150 riders

Our biggest Warsaw event of the season! Often the largest gathering of the year.

With the City official approval: we skate as a group through streets that are normally for cars, where skating usually isn't allowed. We ride safely, with a police escort, and temporarily closed streets, just for us!

It's not a race, more of a relaxed cruise in a big group (dozens, up to ~150 people). Any board welcome: street, surfskate, cruiser, longboard.

Only once or twice per season!

Join
