## About us

Ale Skate Club is a Warsaw-based skateboarding community that has been bringing together skaters from all over the city since 2022.

It doesn’t matter what you ride: a classic deck, a surfskate, longboard, dancing, or even that old board you use to grab groceries. If it has wheels, it fits right in.

**If you can stand on your board and ride straight, come join us!** Whether you’re just starting out or already landing tricks, everyone is welcome.

Our main season runs from April to September, whenever the weather lets us turn Warsaw into our playground. You can find us several times a week cruising by the Vistula river, hanging at local squares, or hitting the skateparks.

We also pull off special events like day & night city rides with a police escort, or crew trips to Skarland — the biggest skatepark in Poland.

Just show up with your board — no ego, no skill-judging — just pure stoke and the joy of riding together!

Our meetups are **100% free** and we use **English** in our official communication because we want everyone to feel welcome — including the international community living in Warsaw. After all, the love for skating has no limits!

## Regular meetings

### Season & logistics (applies to all regular meetups)

Our main season runs from April to September, but the exact dates always depend on the weather. Session times change throughout the season as the days get longer or shorter.

**The rule is simple:** always check our **WhatsApp group** to confirm the current spots and exact times. That’s where the crew stays connected — check the chat before heading out.

---

### ALE Tuesday — River rides

ALE Tuesday River Rides are our **weekly** skate meetups, every **Tuesday** through the season. They’re our core weekday session and bring together the biggest part of the community — if you want to meet as many people from the ALE Skate crew as possible, this is the one.

We start in the city center at **Polna 30a**, next to **Politechnika** metro, and ride toward the **Vistula**. We cruise the riverbanks, see the city from a different angle, and finish at **Letnisko Żoliborz** — deckchairs, drinks, and catching up.

Everyone rides at their own pace on whatever board they like — freedom, good vibes, group energy. We make stops to regroup so nobody gets left behind; beginners are welcome.

**Stay updated:** [WhatsApp group](https://chat.whatsapp.com/LwtLbxt1eqWIBU5DchTXIn?mode=gi_t)

---

### ALE Dance

ALE Dance is our weekly meetup focused on **longboard dancing** — longer boards, flow, style, and movement. Practice, learn, and skate in a more expressive way.

We usually meet in central spots like **Plac Piłsudskiego** or near the **National Stadium**, depending on the day and conditions. Sessions can fall on **different weekdays**; the vibe stays open, welcoming, and supportive.

You’ll find experienced riders and complete beginners. **No matter your level, you’re welcome.**

To join, ask in the **WhatsApp group** when the next dance session is. **No board?** Ask in the group if someone can lend you one for the session.

**Stay updated:** [WhatsApp group](https://chat.whatsapp.com/LwtLbxt1eqWIBU5DchTXIn?mode=gi_t)

---

### ALE Saturday — Zielonka skatepark

Weekly community meetups at the skatepark in **Zielonka**, **Dziennikarska 57**, **every Saturday** from the morning, all season.

Skaters and beginners ride together; sometimes people show up **without a board** just to hang out. We ride, talk, learn from each other, and enjoy being outside. First time at a skatepark? There’s usually someone to show you basics and cheer you on.

Confirm **this Saturday’s** plan and time in the **WhatsApp group**.

**Stay updated:** [WhatsApp group](https://chat.whatsapp.com/LwtLbxt1eqWIBU5DchTXIn?mode=gi_t)

## Special events

### ALE Surfskate Camp in Skarland

Free **weekend** surfskate camps at the **largest skatepark in Poland** in **Skarżysko-Kamienna** (Skarland).

We usually leave **Friday after work** or **early Saturday** and spend the weekend at the park. Days: riding and skating together. Evenings: campfire, **tents on the park grounds**. Good weather: swims in a nearby lake. **3–4 trips per season**; dates are announced **before the season starts**.

**How to join:** WhatsApp — say which event you want; you’ll get meeting point, day, and time. Show up and you’re part of the crew.

**Stay updated:** [WhatsApp group](https://chat.whatsapp.com/LwtLbxt1eqWIBU5DchTXIn?mode=gi_t)

---

### ALE Skateboarding Street Parade — Warsaw (with police escort)

Our **biggest** seasonal event — often the largest turnout.

**With the City of Warsaw:** we skate as a group through **streets that are normally for cars**, where skating usually isn’t allowed — **safely**, with a **police escort**.

Not a race: a **relaxed cruise** in a big group (dozens, up to **~150** people). Freedom and city discovery from board height, not speed.

**Any board** welcome — street, surfskate, longboard, etc.

**Roughly 2 times per season.** Details: **WhatsApp** and **Instagram**.

**Links:**  
[WhatsApp](https://chat.whatsapp.com/LwtLbxt1eqWIBU5DchTXIn?mode=gi_t) · [Instagram](https://www.instagram.com/aleskate.club/)
